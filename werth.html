Werth Institute for Entrepreneurship and Innovation

website redesign | designer & developer

The Peter J. Werth Institute for Entrepreneurship and Innovation links the ecosystem of resources, programs, academic courses, funding, mentorship, education, and activities relating to entrepreneurship and innovation throughout the University of Connecticut.

The Werth Institute wanted a new design for their website to appeal to students and stand out from their competitors. I incorporated their brand standards, remapped their information architecture, and created a more interactive and intuitive experience for students. These are three of the style directions I presented. As of November 2023, the design direction has been selected and approved and I am currently working on the development for it.
