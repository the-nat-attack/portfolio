Alchemist's Fire

prototype | designer

Alchemist's Fire is a prototype for novice Dungeons and Dragons 5E players to create a character. It utilizes a quiz-like format while explaining the steps along the way to help the user understand the character creation process. At the end of the process, it will generate a character sheet based on the user’s responses and random generation. I conducted needfinding before and during development, working to ensure it would meet the users' needs.
