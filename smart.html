Smart Dorms

prototype | designer

With the increase in smart home technology, the idea came to create a "smart dorm" system to act as a smart home designed for the needs of college students in a dorm. I wrote an article on Medium that explains some of the research and design process for Smart Dorms. I also conducted an accessibility review to ensure the technology is accessible and inclusive.
