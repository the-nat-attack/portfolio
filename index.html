Hello! I'm Natalie.

DESIGNER. DEVELOPER. ENTREPRENUER.

Welcome to my portfolio website! This past May, I graduated from New York University with a Master of Science in Integrated Design and Media from the Tandon School of Engineering. I received my B.A. from the University of Connecticut in Digital Media & Design with a concentration in Web/Interactive Media Design. I am passionate about UX & UI design, web development, and all things making. I strive to bridge the gap between humans and technology with accessible and inclusive interfaces. In my free time, I can frequently be found tinkering in the makerspace, getting lost in a good book, or playing Dungeons & Dragons and other tabletop games.
